Spectrum

Spectrum is a safe-space where you can discuss issues, answer questions and learn new things about the LGBTQ+ community and allies. Our goal is to make ISP a better place personally and academically for the students and the staff. We focus on in-school issues, mental health and global events such as equality for transgender people, women's rights etc. We also organise different events around ISP like Ally week. We are open to anyone who wants to attend.

Meetings every Friday in room 235 (Ms Michalsky's room)

How to join

You can drop in anytime during the meetings. If you have any questions you can mail Ms Michalsky (jmichalsky@isp.cz) or Dr. Perry (eperry@isp.cz).
